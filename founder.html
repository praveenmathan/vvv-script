Founder

A R GANDHIKRISHNA

Film Director, Story & Screenplay Writer

A R Gandhikrishna, best known for the Tamil film Chellamae is revered for his directorial expertise, creative vision, mellifluous screenplay and dialogues that have stood the test of time. Thirty years of experience and a name in the film circuit that commands respect, A R Gandhikrishnas journey in the world of cinema began with nothing shy of the term blockbuster. Coming from the Camp of Ace Indian Film Director Shankar S, Gandhikrishna was the pillar behind Shankars blockbuster & magnum opus films like Gentleman, Kaadhalam, Indian, Boys & Anniyan. He has worked in close tandem with the ace screenwriter, storywriter and master storyteller Sujatha on these projects and their collaboration continued further as A R Gandhikrishan started out on his directorial ventures.

A R Gandhikrishnas directorial debut Nila Kaalam received widespread acclamation and awards. Anandha Thandavam, his next was a Film adaptation of renowned writer Sujathas serialised novel Pirivom Santhippom. A R Gandhikrishna engages the audience with substance and style; revels in handling a sensitive screenplay of strong emotions with artistry techniques as quoted by critics and industry patrons is but a reflection of his innate temperance and style.

The Films list; in which the pivotal contribution of AR Gandhikrishna is immensely acknowledged by the Film fraternity includes Indian 2, Anegan, Endhiran (Telugu), Shivaji: The Boss, Anniyan, Boys, Nayak, Jeans, 12 B, Ullam ketkumae, Kana Kandaen, Karikalan, Kaapaan. He has also collaborated frequently with eminent names like Jeeva & KV Anand.

A R Gandhikrishna helmed the prestigious film Engineer, with Arvind Swamy and Madhuri Dixit in the lead, music by A R Rahman, Cinematography handled by the ace Cameraman Jeeva, with a farsighted theme was an ambitious film that created a lot of buzz in the Indian Film Circuit. Madhuri Dixit, the leading actress in Hindi cinema in the 1990s, played the lead role for the First time in a South Indian movie in Engineer.

With a commanding voice in deconstructing a story in all its layers and thirty years of experience backing his trained hand in handling a screenplay and construction of a film project in all its glorious forms with sheer precision and vision, A R Gandhikrishna is now looking forward to collaborate with the directors, screenwriters and the visionaries of the film fraternity, striding forward in his mission towards pushing the boundaries of storytelling and the meticulous shaping up of a Film Project.
